The 5 Questions Per Role (from MEMORY.md)
Agent Questions
  1. AUT_A1 — I perform better when I can decide how to approach my work.
  2. COM_A1 — I feel unsettled if I go long periods without specific feedback.
  3. REL_A1 — Extended periods of working independently reduce my motivation.
  4. GRIT_A1 — I continue working toward goals even when progress is slow.
  5. SR_A1 — I follow a consistent structure when completing my responsibilities.
Broker Questions
  1. AUT_B1 — This brokerage allows individuals to decide how they approach their work.
  2. COM_B1 — This brokerage provides specific actionable feedback.
  3. REL_B1 — This brokerage fosters meaningful connection among people.
  4. GRIT_B1 — Agents here are expected to maintain consistent outreach during slow periods.
  5. SR_B1 — Clear weekly activity expectations are defined for agents here.
